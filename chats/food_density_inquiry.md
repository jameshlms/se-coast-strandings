| Dataset | Why it indicates food density | Citation |
|--------|-------------------------------|----------|
| **SAHFOS CPR (Continuous Plankton Recorder)** | Long-term, monthly plankton counts across the North Atlantic ‚Äî a gold standard for zooplankton abundance. |  |
